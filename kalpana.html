TIME LINE OF KALPANA CHAWLA'S LIFE

  • 17 March 1962 -Kalpana Chawla was born on 17 March 1962 in Karnal, a city located in the Indian state of Haryana.
  • 1978 -In 1978, she completed her schooling from DAV College and decided to enter Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh, with Aeronautical Engineering. While her father objected to the idea, believing teaching or medicine to be more suitable career option for a girl, her mother gave her unfaltering support. Eventually, her father relented.
  • 1982 -1982, Kalpana earned her Bachelors degree in Aeronautical Engineering, securing the third rank in her batch. With it, she became the first female aeronautical engineer to pass out from Punjab Engineering College.
  • 1984 - Kalpana graduated from master degree in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Texas in USA.
  • 1996 - Chawla was assigned to Space Shuttle Columbia flight, STS-87 as Mission Specialist 1 and the primary Robotic Arm Operator. It was launched on November 19, 1997 from Launch Complex 39B of the Kennedy Space Center.
